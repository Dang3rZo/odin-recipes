Easy Delicious Homemade Pizza

This is an easy to follow recipe for everyones favorite food, PIZZA!

We will provide a basic cheese pizza recipe, but toppings are entirely up to you!

What are some of your favorite toppings?

Ingredients

  • dough
  • sauce
  • cheese

Steps

  1. Spread the dough on a baking sheet
  2. Add your sauce
  3. Top with cheese
  4. Bake at 400 degrees for 20 minutes or untill the cheese is melted and golden brown
